How to Play

  • Select your Hoverboard from the Loadout Station.
  • Gear up with Armor Sets and Power Perks.
  • Join a team and assign your role in the Team Builder.
  • Hit the arena through Free Skate or Match Mode.
  • Score goals using the HoverCore ball and hover stick.
  • Use ramps and walls for tricks and speed boosts.
  • Coordinate with your team to dominate the match!
