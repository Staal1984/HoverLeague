📘 How to Play

🎮 Objective

Score more goals than the opposing team using your hoverboard, HoverCore ball, and Hover Stick. Matches are fast, team-based, and played in futuristic hover arenas.

🏟️ Match Format

  • 5v5 Teams: 5 players per team
  • 3 Rounds: Each round is 5 minutes long
  • Sudden Death Overtime if scores are tied
  • 1 Goal = 1 Point

🛹 Roles & Classes

  • Striker ⚽ – Main goal scorer, high speed
  • Defender 🛡️ – Protects the goal zone
  • Support 🔧 – Assists teammates, clears paths
  • Scout 🕵️ – Fastest player, retrieves ball quickly
  • Enforcer 💥 – Physical player, blocks/tackles
  • Tech Specialist 🧠 – Unlocks advanced control + upgrades (League-level only)

🕹️ Controls

  • Tap or press to boost, pass, shoot, and activate tricks
  • Mobile: On-screen buttons
  • PC: Keyboard (WASD, spacebar, arrow keys)
  • Hover Stick: Use to extend reach and control HoverCore

🔋 Power-Ups & Gear

  • Collect boost pads in the arena
  • Equip custom gear from the Loadout screen
  • Upgrade hoverboards, sticks, and armor for better stats

📈 Leveling Up

  • Earn XP for goals, assists, tackles, and wins
  • Unlock new stadiums, gear, and skills
  • Customize your team and player card
