How to Play Hover League

🏁 Objective

Hover League is a futuristic sport combining hoverboards, teamwork, and high-speed strategy. The goal is to score more points than the other team using the HoverCore™ ball.

🎮 Controls

  • Mobile: Tap buttons for pass, shoot, boost, and tricks.
  • PC: Use WASD to move, space to jump, and number keys for abilities.

🏟️ Field Layout

  • Center Zone: Neutral play area
  • Goal Zones: Score by getting the HoverCore into the opposing goal
  • Ramp Zones: Gain vertical advantage or speed

🧠 Roles

Choose from different player classes:

  • Striker ⚽: Fast and aggressive, focused on scoring goals
  • Defender 🛡️: Protects the goal zone and blocks attacks
  • Support 🔧: Clears paths and assists teammates
  • Scout 🕵️: Agile and quick, ideal for fast retrieval
  • Enforcer 💥: Powerful impact player who disrupts enemy plays
  • Tech Specialist 🧠: Controls hover stick and advanced tools

⏱️ Match Rules

  • 5v5 Matches
  • 3 x 5-minute rounds
  • 1 Point per Goal
  • Sudden Death Overtime if tied
