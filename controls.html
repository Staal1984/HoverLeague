Controls + Tips

Master your hoverboard with these controls and quick tips before entering the arena.

📱 Mobile Controls

  • 🔼 Swipe Up – Boost
  • ⬅️➡️ Tilt – Steer left/right
  • 👆 Tap – Shoot or Trick
  • 👆👆 Double Tap – Power move

🖥️ PC Controls

  • W – Forward
  • A / D – Turn left / right
  • S – Quick stop
  • Spacebar – Boost
  • Mouse Click – Interact / Shoot
  • 1–4 – Use Power Perks

🔥 Pro Tips

  • Hold Boost while turning for drift-style maneuvers
  • Double tap to chain power perks with tricks
  • Play Free Skate mode to master the controls
